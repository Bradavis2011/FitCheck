## Full Description (both stores)

Ever stood in front of the mirror unsure if your outfit actually works? Or This? gives you instant, honest feedback in seconds — so you never leave the house wondering.

Snap a photo of your outfit, tell us the occasion, and get AI-powered style feedback covering fit, color coordination, proportions, and overall vibe. It's like having a personal stylist in your pocket, available 24/7.

HOW IT WORKS
1. Take a photo or choose one from your gallery
2. Tell us where you're headed (work, date night, casual, event)
3. Get a detailed score and actionable style tips in seconds
4. Ask follow-up questions for deeper advice

KEY FEATURES
- AI-powered outfit analysis with detailed scoring
- Personalized feedback on fit, color, proportion, and style
- Follow-up questions for deeper styling advice
- Community feedback from real people
- Style DNA profile that evolves with you
- Outfit history to track your style journey
- Privacy-first: blur your face, control who sees your outfits
- Gamification: earn points, badges, and climb the leaderboard

SUBSCRIPTION TIERS
- Free: 3 outfit checks per day
- Plus: Unlimited checks + community features
- Pro: Unlimited + priority AI processing

Perfect for job interviews, date nights, special events, or just nailing your everyday look.
